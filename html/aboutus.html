About Us

"Foodiess is a food ordering and delivery company based out of Mumbai, India. Foodiess was inspired by the thought of providing a complete food ordering and delivery solution to the urban foodie. A single window for ordering from a wide range of menu, we have our own exclusive fleet of delivery personnel to pickup orders from restaurants and deliver it to customers . Having our own fleet gives us the flexibility to offer customers a no minimum order policy on our restaurant and accept online payments. Our delivery personnel carry one order at a time which ensures we get reliable and fast deliveries."
